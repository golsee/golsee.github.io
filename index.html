Welcome to Golsee's Birthday Wishlist! Golsee's birthday is on November 28th. Although she has wants, she often finds herself not thinking they are necessary so she doesn't really keep track of them anymore unfortunately (or fortunately?). Anways, she decided to create this website to keep track of some things she has found that she is interested in and to help her friends and family out when deciding on a gift for her.
